PRIVACY & DATA PROTECTION

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 2026

1. Overview & Local-First Philosophy

Librixa is designed from the ground up as a personal, local-first audiobook library manager and player. We believe your listening history, personal audio files, and reading habits belong solely to you. Librixa operates without registration, accounts, or mandatory cloud infrastructure.

2. Google Drive Integration & Access

When you choose to connect your Google Drive account within Librixa:

  • Read-Only Access: Librixa requests read-only permissions specifically restricted to the audio files and folders that you explicitly select or grant access to for streaming and downloading audiobooks.
  • No Centralized Storage: Librixa does not upload, transfer, copy, or index your Google Drive files to any third-party or proprietary servers.
  • Direct Authorization: Authentication is handled directly between your device and Google's OAuth 2.0 authorization servers. Librixa never receives or stores your Google password.

3. Data Collection & Local Storage

Librixa collects no personal data beyond what is strictly required for local app functionality on your Android device:

  • Playback History & Bookmarks: Your listening progress, chapter bookmarks, sleep timer preferences, and playback statistics are stored 100% locally on your device storage.
  • No Analytics or Tracking: Librixa contains zero third-party analytics SDKs, advertising trackers, or telemetry tools. We do not track what audiobooks you listen to or when you listen to them.
  • Data Portability: You can export your listening history, playlists, and settings as a local configuration file (.lxb) at any time, allowing you to back up or transfer your data across devices independently.

4. Third-Party Services

Librixa does not share, sell, or monetize any user data. The only external connection established by the app occurs when you enable Google Drive streaming, in which case communication takes place directly between your Android device and Google APIs.
